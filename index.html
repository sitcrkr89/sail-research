HBM4 transition thesis

Verified fact · Primary sources
All three major HBM vendors have live first-party disclosures establishing an HBM4 product, demonstration, production, or shipment position.

This supports the narrow conclusion that the generation transition is active. It does not establish which vendor leads on customer qualification, volume, yield, or economics.

Decision implication

Shift monitoring from roadmap existence toward qualification, volume, yield, packaging, and customer-specific deployment evidence.
